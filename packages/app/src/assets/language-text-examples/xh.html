Septemba 2, 2078

Abasayensi baxelele imfumaneko emangalisayo kwicwangciso leSaturn, iTitan: uhlobo olutsha lwempilo olufana ne-algae. Le nkuthazo yenziwa ngumsebenzisi Titan Explorer, owaqoqa izithole kwizizi ze-methane ne-ethane yamanzi. Ezi zinhlobo zempilo ezifana ne-algae zinakho ukuphila kwiimeko ezibandayo kakhulu apho kwakungalindelwanga nasiphina isiphiliso. Le nkuthazo iyaguqula indlela abasayensi abajonge ngayo ubomi kulo mhlaba wonke kwaye ibonisa ukuba izidalwa eziphilayo zinokuhlala kwiindawo ezahlukileyo kakhulu neMhlaba.

Abochwepheshe bathi le nkuthazo inokusinceda siqonde indlela obomi baqala ngayo ehlabathini lethu. Ukuba i-algae inokuphila eziqwini ezigqilelwe zeTitan, ubomi unokuhlala namanye amaplanethi nemiphando ekude. Isinyathelo esilandelayo kukufunda isakhiwo segqatsi sezidalwa ezi nokubheka ukukhula kwazo. Abasayensi sele belungiselela amaphulo aseleyo ukuya eTitan ukuze bafunde okungakumbi ngezi zinhlobo zempilo ezimangalisayo. Oku kubonisa ukuqala isahluko esitsha ekuphenyeni indawo.
