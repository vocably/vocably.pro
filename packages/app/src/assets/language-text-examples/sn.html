Gunyana 2, 2078

Vanamatongerwa vapazivisa kuchonekwa kukuru pana mwedzi weSaturn, Titan: chimiro chitsva chehupenyu chakafanana nealgae. Kuchonekwa kwakaitwa nechishandiso Titan Explorer, chakaunganidza zvitsanzo zvemvura yemethane naethane yakaora. Izvi zvikamu zvehupenyu zvakafanana nealgae zvinogona kukura muzvimbo zvinyoro nyoro kwazvo apo hakuna humwe hupenyu hwaitarisirwa. Kuchonekwa uku kunochinja nzira yavanamatomberwa vanoona hupenyu munyika yose uye chinotarisira kuti zvipenyu zvinogona kuvapo muzvimbo zvakasiyana zvikuru neNyika.

Nyanzvi dzinoti kuchonekwa uku kunogona kutibatsira kunzwisisa kuti hupenyu hwatanga sei panyika yedu. Kana algae inogona kukura mumadhamu akasvimha aTitan, hupenyu hunogona kuvapo pamwe papurazi uye mwedzi uri kure. Danho rinotevera kudzidza chimiro cheganda cheganda chezvipfupi izvi uye kuona kukura kwazvo. Vanamatomberwa vari kutungamira zvimbo zvitsva kuenda kuna Titan kuti vadzidzire zvimwe nezve zvikamu zvitsva zvehupenyu izvi. Izvi zvinoratidza kutanga kwechitsauko chitsva mukurangarira nzvimbo.
